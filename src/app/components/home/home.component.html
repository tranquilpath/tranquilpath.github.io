What Services are Included

Retirement & Life Planning

We help you discover the ways to use your money and other resources to create an amazing life while ensuring that you are not running out of money.

Investments

Our investment approach creates a portfolio of bonds and low-cost mutual funds based on your cash flow needs now and in retirement. This creates a portfolio that is unique to you, your needs, and your circumstances.

Tax Planning

Taxes touch every portion of your plan. In addition to making tax conscious decisions throughout your plan, we set aside time each year to create a strategy to optimize your taxes now and in the future.

Special Needs Planning

Many of our clients either have a disability or have a family member with a disability. For these clients, we not only consider the ramifications throughout the plan, we frequently set aside meetings specifically to discuss goals and strategies specific to those needs.

Insurance

We advise on insurance of all varieties, from car insurance to disability insurance. We provide guidance on how to protect your future without trying to sell you policies.

Estate Planning

We work with your estate attorney to ensure that your estate planning documents work well with your financial situation.
